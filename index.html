Delivery flow & milestones

Typical embedded and firmware engagements move through a phased flow, with concrete milestones and handover points.

  • Phase 0 – Discovery: clarify requirements, hardware constraints, risks, and define the initial milestone plan.
  • Phase 1 – Bring‑up: board power‑on, boot to shell/RTOS, core peripherals enabled, initial diagnostics and logs.
  • Phase 2 – Features: implement and validate feature sets in small, testable milestones (e.g. “connectivity ready”, “sensing & control ready”).
  • Phase 3 – Release: stabilisation, test evidence, documentation, and handover of production firmware images.
  • Phase 4 – Support: ongoing fixes, enhancements, and new variants via retainer or fractional engagement.
